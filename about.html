BIO

After many years in academia, teaching high school and college students, I desired new challenges to hone other skills and contribute to society in new ways. I decided to dedicate myself to learning to code at Denver's Turing School of Software and Design. This opportunity gave me the skills to enter today's software development workforce, with a focus on Test Driven Development, Agile work practices, and workflow best practices.

On top of my passion for technology, my personal interests include performing music and helping others learn new skills, such as coding. We're all in this together, and I believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork.
